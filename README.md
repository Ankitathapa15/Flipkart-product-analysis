# 🛍️ Flipkart Product Analysis

## 📖 Project Overview

The **Flipkart Product Analysis** project explores product data from Flipkart to uncover meaningful business insights using **Python, SQL, and Tableau**. The project involves data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL-based querying, and the development of an interactive Tableau dashboard for visualizing key metrics and trends.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Clean and preprocess the Flipkart product dataset.
* Analyze pricing and discount patterns.
* Identify top-performing brands and product categories.
* Compare retail prices with discounted prices.
* Build an interactive Tableau dashboard to support business decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Python** – Pandas, NumPy
* **SQL** – Data querying and analysis
* **Tableau** – Interactive dashboard creation
* **Jupyter Notebook** – Data cleaning and exploratory analysis

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The Tableau dashboard includes the following visualizations:

- 📦 Total Products – Displays the total number of products available in the dataset.
- 💰 Average Selling Price – Shows the average discounted selling price of all products.
- 🎁 Average Discount Percentage – Highlights the average discount offered across products.
- 🏷️ Top Brands – Displays the top 10 brands based on the number of listed products.
- 📂 Products by Category – Visualizes the distribution of products across different categories.
- 📉 Top 10 Brands by Average Discount – Compares brands offering the highest average discounts.
- 🛍️ Top 10 Discounted Products – Identifies products with the highest discounted prices.
- ✅ Flipkart Advantage Distribution – Shows the proportion of products eligible for Flipkart Advantage using a pie chart.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Significant discounts are offered across various product categories.
* A few brands dominate the product catalog.
* Most products belong to the low and medium price ranges.
* A few brands contribute a significant share of the total product listings, with **Allure Auto** leading in the number of available products.
* **Furniture**, **Jewellery**, and **Clothing** are among the most populated product categories, reflecting their strong presence on the platform.
* The **Flipkart Advantage** feature is available for only a small percentage of products, while the majority of products are not Flipkart Advantage eligible.
* Premium products, such as luxury watches and accessories, appear among the **Top 10 Discounted Products**, indicating that high-value items also receive substantial discounts.
